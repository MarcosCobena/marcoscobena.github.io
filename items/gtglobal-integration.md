As I had some experience with .NET in WebAssembly, my manager asked me to join this project.
My goal has been to integrate a third-party platform in CEINOR's Genes VNext.
CEINOR is a Spanish company that provides ERP solutions to repair shops.
Genes VNext is their latest iteration moving from Windows Forms (desktop) to Blazor (Web).
It is one of the largest codebases I have ever worked on,
backed with a SQL Server database where everything is stored.
Thanks to Javier García, one of the CEINOR employees, I have been able to understand how things work under the hood.
My approach has been to rely on unit testing for the most critical parts,
also to ease debugging since end-to-end scenarios required a bunch of steps.
